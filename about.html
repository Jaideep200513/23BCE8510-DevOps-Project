Our Origin

Born From a Passion to Build Better Technology

NexaTech Solutions was founded in 2012 by four MIT graduates who believed that enterprise software didn't have to be slow, fragile, or prohibitively expensive. Armed with deep expertise in distributed systems and cloud infrastructure, they set out to change how businesses interact with technology.

What began as a cloud consulting boutique in San Francisco quickly evolved as client after client discovered what NexaTech could deliver: architectures that actually scaled, AI that genuinely worked, and security postures that held up against sophisticated adversaries.

Today, NexaTech operates from 8 global offices, serves 200+ enterprise clients across 40 countries, and employs over 850 of the most talented technology professionals in the industry.

Milestones

Our Journey Through the Years

From a garage startup to a globally respected technology leader — here are the defining moments.

2012

NexaTech is Founded

Four MIT graduates launch NexaTech Solutions in a shared San Francisco coworking space, landing their first three cloud consulting contracts within 60 days.

2014

Series A – $12M Funding

Andreessen Horowitz leads a $12M Series A round. NexaTech expands to 45 employees and opens offices in New York and Austin.

2016

AI Practice Launch

NexaTech establishes its dedicated AI/ML practice, hiring 30 PhDs and research engineers. First AI product goes live with a Fortune 500 retailer.

2018

Global Expansion

NexaTech opens offices in London, Singapore, and Dubai. Client roster grows to 80 enterprises. Named among Deloitte's Technology Fast 500.

2020

500+ Employees & Pandemic Pivot

NexaTech achieves 500-employee milestone. Rapidly pivots to help 40+ clients build remote-first infrastructure during the global pandemic.

2022

Series C – $120M & Gartner Recognition

$120M Series C funding at $800M valuation. Named Gartner Cool Vendor in Cloud Infrastructure. Launches NexaAI — a proprietary enterprise GenAI platform.

2025

850+ Team Members & Beyond

NexaTech reaches 850+ employees, 200+ enterprise clients, and $240M ARR. Continues expanding AI and quantum computing R&D capabilities.
