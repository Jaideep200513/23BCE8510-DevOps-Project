FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to questions we often receive from prospective clients and partners.

How quickly can NexaTech start a new project? +

After an initial discovery call and contract signing, we typically begin onboarding within 2–4 weeks. For urgent engagements, we can often accelerate this to 5–7 business days by pre-assigning a dedicated team from our bench of available engineers.

Do you work with startups or only large enterprises? +

Our primary focus is enterprise clients (Series B+ companies and large organizations), but we do selectively work with early-stage startups that have a compelling technical challenge. We recommend connecting with our team to discuss your specific situation.

What engagement models do you offer? +

We offer three primary engagement models: (1) Fixed-Price Projects for well-defined scopes, (2) Time & Materials for evolving requirements, and (3) Dedicated Team arrangements where we embed NexaTech engineers directly within your organization.

Is my data and IP protected when working with NexaTech? +

Absolutely. All client engagements are governed by comprehensive NDAs and MSAs. You retain 100% ownership of all IP, code, and data produced during our engagement. We are ISO 27001 certified and SOC 2 Type II compliant.

Do you provide post-launch support and maintenance? +

Yes. All projects include a 90-day warranty period post-launch. We also offer flexible support retainers with 24/7 on-call options, SLA guarantees, and dedicated support engineers for mission-critical systems.
