Our Process

A Fair, Transparent Hiring Process

We respect your time and aim to make every step of the hiring process clear, structured, and human.

01

Apply Online

Submit your application with CV and portfolio. Our recruiting team reviews every application within 5 business days.

02

Recruiter Call

A 30-minute call with our Talent team to discuss your background, the role requirements, and answer your questions.

03

Technical Interview

Two technical interviews focused on real-world problem-solving relevant to the role. No leetcode-style puzzle tricks.

04

Offer & Onboarding

A competitive offer with full transparency. A structured 90-day onboarding program sets you up for success from day one.
